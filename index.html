SOUND OF ANEMOIA
RETROWAVE GATHER 2023

The annual gathering of synth music enthusiasts is returning for its fourth year, featuring an exciting lineup of speakers, live performances, a music gallery, project crafting workshops, and networking opportunities. Join us for a weekend of exploring the latest gear, techniques, and sounds in the world of electronic music. Don't miss out on this incredible opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals and take your passion for synth music to the next level.
